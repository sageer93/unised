Our Impact

UNISED is a non-profit ed-tech organization working to bring equitable access to quality education pan-India partnering with state governments, several IITs, schools and parents. We work to strengthen Science and Math education between grades 6-12, promote digital learning in government schools, conduct teacher training workshops and implement various education policies of Central and State governments.
